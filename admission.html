OUR ADMISSION PROCESS

**Prostective students are to obtain the school's admission form online, or in the schools administrative office.

**Look out for the dates of entrance examination which will be executed before selection.

**Admission letters for successful candidates will be made available in the candidates email or the school administrative office.

**Every successful candidate will be required to pay tuition and other spelt out fees which will be attached to the admission letter.

DOCUMENTATION

Prospective students will be required to submit the following for registretion and documentation purpose:

**A photocopy of birth certificate.

**A photocopy of certificate of state of origin.

**Two recent passport photocopy.

**Certificate of medical fitness or record of any medical history that requires special attention.

**Last result from previous school for students on transfer.
