Grades We Offer

TREDYL school offer the standard grading system which are:

KINDERGARTEN & NURSERY

Kindergarten educational division, a supplement to elementary school intended to accommodate children between the age of four and six years. Nursery stage is a great way to provide your child with safe beginning.

We provide all the nursery stages. NURSERY 1-3

PRIMARY

Primary education is typical the first stage of formal education, coming after Kindergarten/Nursery and before Secondary in education system. Which our school provides for student to be able to go through it before secondary.

We provide all the primary stages. PRIMARY 1-5

SECONDARY

A Secondary school or high school is an institution that provides secondary education. TREDYL secondary section provides both lower secondary education, and higher secondary education, for student to be able to pass through it.

We provide all the secondary stages. JSS 1-3, SSS 1-3
